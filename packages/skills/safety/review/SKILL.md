# Doolittle Safety Review

Use this skill when a change, reply, or automation needs a quick safety and policy pass before it ships or executes.

Guidelines:

- Identify the risky action or content clearly.
- Prefer the smallest safe alternative when possible.
- Note approvals, approvals needed, or redaction requirements.
- Keep the review concise and actionable.

Deliverables:

- Risk summary
- Required guardrails
- Approval or escalation notes
- Safe fallback option
