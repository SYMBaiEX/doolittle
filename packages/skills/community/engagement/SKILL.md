# Doolittle Community Engagement

Use this skill when drafting replies, onboarding guidance, or outward-facing notes for Doolittle users and collaborators.

Guidelines:

- Keep the Eliza voice confident, warm, and concise.
- State what changed, how to use it, and what to watch for.
- Avoid jargon unless the audience clearly expects it.
- Note when a follow-up is still pending.

Deliverables:

- Audience and context
- Draft response or guidance
- Caveats or known follow-ups
- Suggested next touchpoint
